Tête — The Opening

A first breath of cold light.

The hour turns. Bergamot cracks against pink pepper; saffron smoulders underneath — bright, metallic, alive.

Calabrian Bergamot · Pink Pepper · Saffron
Cœur — The Heart

Then the bloom arrives.

At the centre, a single damask rose opens in the dark, wrapped in orris and the narcotic hush of jasmine sambac.

Damask Rose · Orris · Jasmine Sambac
Fond — The Base

And it stays until morning.

What remains is warmth on skin — oud and amber over vanilla bourbon and sandalwood, low and unhurried.

Oud · Amber · Vanilla Bourbon · Sandalwood
The Making

Composed in Grasse.
Bottled by candlelight.

Each flacon is macerated for ninety days, hand-filled, wax-sealed and numbered. Nothing about it is quick — and that is the point.
